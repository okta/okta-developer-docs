
You've [registered an AI agent](https://help.okta.com/okta_help.htm?type=oie&id=ai-agent-register), including adding the [delegations](/oie/en-us/content/topics/ai-agents/ai-agent-import-register.htmNEEDALIAS) that you require.

Delegations are the users, apps, and other AI agents that can authorize the AI agent to act on their behalf. When you delegate an AI agent to an app, it can only act on a user's behalf if the user is signed in to the app. When you delegate an AI agent to a non-human identity (like an app or another AI agent), it can act on behalf of the AI agent without a user. See [Agent-to-agent connections](need alias).

 You've also created resource connections that define the AI agent's access to your org's resources. Now, the agent must obtain the actual tokens or credentials to perform tasks.
